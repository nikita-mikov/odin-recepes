Creamy Mushroom Chicken

Description

This recipe is a simple and flavorful dish that comes together in no time. The creamy mushroom sauce paired with the tender chicken makes for a comforting meal that is perfect for a weeknight dinner. You can serve it over pasta, rice or mashed potatoes to make it a complete meal. It's a dish that can be enjoyed by the whole family and can be easily adjusted to suit different dietary needs.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then coat them in flour.
  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat the olive oil. Add the chicken breasts and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side or until golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the onion, mushrooms and garlic. Cook for about 5 minutes, or until the mushrooms are softened and the onions are translucent.
  4. Stir in the chicken broth and bring to a simmer. Cook for about 2-3 minutes, or until the liquid has reduced by about half.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream and bring the mixture to a simmer. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  6. Return the chicken to the skillet and spoon the sauce over the chicken.
  7. Sprinkle with chopped parsley and serve hot.
