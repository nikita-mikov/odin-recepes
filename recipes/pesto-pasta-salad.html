Pesto Pasta Salad

Description

This pasta salad is a great option for a summer side dish or a light lunch. The homemade pesto sauce gives it a fresh and flavorful taste, and the cherry tomatoes add a pop of color and a touch of sweetness. It's a simple recipe but it packs a lot of flavor and can be served cold or at room temperature, making it a great option for a picnic or a potluck.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of pasta (your choice of shape)
  • 1 cup of fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup of pine nuts
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pint of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup of chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  2. In a food processor or blender, pulse together the basil, Parmesan cheese, pine nuts, and garlic until well combined. With the motor running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. In a large bowl, toss the pasta with the pesto sauce until well coated.
  4. Add the cherry tomatoes and parsley, and toss again.
  5. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes or up to 2 hours to let the flavors meld together.
  6. Serve cold and enjoy!
