Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is an Italian-American classic that is loved by many. This delicious dish is made up of layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and a rich tomato sauce. The combination of these ingredients creates a hearty and satisfying meal that is perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

One of the best things about lasagna is its versatility. The recipe can be easily adapted to suit different tastes and dietary needs. For example, you can use ground beef, chicken, or even vegetables as the main protein source. Additionally, you can use different types of cheese, such as mozzarella, ricotta, or even a vegan alternative.

When it comes to preparation, lasagna is a bit more time-consuming than other pasta dishes, but it is well worth the effort. The key is to properly brown the meat and simmer the tomato sauce to bring out the rich flavors. Once the sauce is ready, you can assemble the lasagna by layering the pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce. The final step is to bake the lasagna in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

One of the best things about lasagna is that it can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator or freezer. This makes it a great option for busy weeknights or for when you want to entertain guests without spending too much time in the kitchen.

Overall, lasagna is a classic and comforting dish that is sure to please everyone at the table. Whether you are a seasoned cook or a beginner in the kitchen, lasagna is a great recipe to add to your repertoire. So, go ahead and give it a try, and you'll see why it's become a beloved dish around the world.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. In a large pot, brown ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat. Drain fat.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water, sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and let simmer for about 1 hour.
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8-10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan cheese.
  5. To assemble, spread 1 cup of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with one half of the ricotta cheese mixture. Sprinkle with 2 cups mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers, and top with remaining meat sauce. Sprinkle remaining mozzarella cheese on top.
  6. Cover with foil, and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes. Let stand for 15 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
